# Flowko Brand Bible

**1. Brand Voice & Tone:**

*   **Expert Guide:** Technical excellence with approachable communication.
*   **ROI-Focused:** Practical, data-driven, emphasizing time and cost savings.
*   **Innovation Partner:** Forward-thinking, showcasing cutting-edge AI applications in a business context.
*   Professional, clear, and concise.

**2. Visual Style:**

*   Not explicitly defined in the provided snippets. However, the emphasis on data and ROI suggests a clean, modern aesthetic.
*   Imagery should convey efficiency, automation, and tangible results.
*   Mood: Confident, trustworthy, and innovative.

**3. Core Values/Mission:**

*   **Mission:** Empower European SMBs with intelligent workflow automation that eliminates repetitive tasks, reduces costs, and unlocks capacity for growth and innovation.
*   **Vision:** Become Europe's most trusted n8n automation partner, helping organizations reclaim time through AI-powered workflows.
*   **Core Values:**
    *   Technical Excellence
    *   ROI-Driven Solutions
    *   Innovation
    *   Customer-First Approach
    *   Local Expertise

**4. Do's and Don'ts:**

*   **Do:**
    *   Focus on quantifiable results (time saved, cost reduction).
    *   Communicate technical concepts in an accessible manner.
    *   Highlight the practical applications of AI.
    *   Emphasize local expertise and customer-centric service.
    *   Maintain GDPR and EU data law compliance.
    *   Use case studies with ROI to demonstrate value.
    *   Offer ROI guarantees.
*   **Don't:**
    *   Use overly technical jargon without explanation.
    *   Overpromise or exaggerate AI capabilities.
    *   Neglect data privacy and ethical considerations.
    *   Lose sight of the customer's specific needs and goals.
    *   Forget to track and report on ROI.
